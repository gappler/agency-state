# What to Expect in Session 1

You've done the prework. Session 1 is Monday, June 8, 10am ET. Here's the shape of what's coming.

**Read time:** ~5 minutes.

---

## What Session 1 is for

Session 1 does three things:

1. Seeds your knowledge base using the prework files you've already produced
2. Walks you through building your first two outputs — a performance dashboard and a written report
3. Establishes the method you'll practice all cohort: **context → conversation → outputs**

By the end, you'll have a project folder with your brand definition and synthetic data in place, and two working outputs that read from your context.

**Claude Code install is prework, not session work.** You should arrive with `claude --version` returning a version number. We don't spend session time on installs — we spend it on building.

---

## The shape of the 2 hours

The session is structured in clear phases. You'll always know where you are.

| Phase | Time | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Project setup** | 15 min | Create your project folder, set up `CLAUDE.md`, drop in your prework files |
| **Quick intros** | 5 min | One sentence each: who's the company, what's the data |
| **Understand** | 10 min | Walk through how to write a prompt that tells Claude Code what to do |
| **Build: performance dashboard** | 30 min | Build from your data using a reference prompt |
| **Reflect** | 15 min | What did your tool produce? What would you change? |
| **Build: written report** | 30 min | A second tool that reads from your data and produces a narrative interpretation |
| **Wrap and preview** | 15 min | What we built today, what's next tomorrow |

Most of the session runs in the main room as a guided group. If you hit a deeper stuck point during a build phase, I'll send you to a breakout with the reference materials and check in.

---

## What to have ready

- Your `brand_definition.md` and `[yourcompany]-data.csv` saved in your prework folder
- Claude Code installed and working: `claude --version` returns a version number
- Claude Pro subscription active on your account
- Zoom installed and updated, stable internet
- 2 uninterrupted hours

---

## What we handle live (so you don't have to)

- **The `CLAUDE.md` file.** From a template we'll give you — you'll copy, paste, and lightly edit.
- **The first prompts.** Reference prompts are provided. You modify them to fit your company.
- **Anything that breaks during a build.** Breakout rooms during the session; Wednesday office hours as backup.

---

## Things that might go sideways (and how we handle them)

- **Your install broke overnight.** Uncommon but it happens. Run `claude --version` before we start; if it fails, Slack DM me right before class or come anyway and we'll triage.
- **Your prework is incomplete.** Come anyway. We improvise.
- **Your first build doesn't work the first time.** Expected. The reflect phase is where we figure out what to change. Iterating is the skill.
- **Claude Code feels unfamiliar.** Also expected. I screen-share each step; you follow along in your own terminal.

---

## Emotional expectations

This isn't a test. Nobody's grading your brand definition or your CSV. The point is to get you building — and to let you experience what it feels like when context and conversation produce real outputs for *your* company.

If you feel uncertain at the start, that's normal. Most people feel fine inside the first 10 minutes of actual building. The learning happens through doing, not through understanding everything first.

---

- **Tuesday, June 9 (Session 2):** same time, same Zoom link. We build planning and decision outputs.
- **Wednesday, June 10 (office hours):** optional, drop-in. Use it if Session 1 left you with a lingering install issue, or if you want to get a head start on Session 2 concepts.

See you Monday.
