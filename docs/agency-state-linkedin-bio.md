## About section

I've spent 20+ years building and leading marketing teams — across agencies (Young & Rubicam, Magnet/AKQA, Designory, Grafik) and a decade running marketing at The American Institute of Architects. At AIA, I grew a 3-person team into a 12-person department, launched AIA's first continuing education platform, and drove membership to its highest point in the organization's history.

Now I run Agency State, which teaches business and marketing professionals to build working tools with Claude Code — content engines, forecasting models, campaign reports, landing pages, media plans — using their own brand, data, and strategy. No programming background required.

The methodology: capture what your team knows, build tools sequentially from that foundation, keep humans in the loop at every stage. Four live demo suites at agencystate.ai. Founding cohorts starting June 2026.
