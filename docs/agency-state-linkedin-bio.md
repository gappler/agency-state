## About section

20+ years building and leading marketing teams at digital agencies (Young & Rubicam, Magnet/AKQA, Designory, Grafik) and a decade at The American Institute of Architects. At AIA, I grew a 3-person team into a 12-person department, launched AIA's first continuing education platform, and drove conference attendance and membership to their highest levels in the organization's history.

Now I run Agency State, which teaches business and marketing innovators to build working tools with Claude Code. Strategic plans, dashboards, content engines, reports, even websites — using their own brand, data, and strategy. No programming background required.

The methodology: start with business context, build tools from that foundation, keep humans in the loop at every stage. See demos at agencystate.ai.
