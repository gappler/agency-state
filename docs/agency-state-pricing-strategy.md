# Agency State — Pricing Strategy

## Cohort-Based Intensives

### Format
- 4 sessions × 2 hours across 2 weeks
- Live online via Zoom (or similar)
- 8–12 participants per cohort
- Office hours between sessions (30 min, optional)
- Shared Slack channel for peer support during and after

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Founding Cohorts (first 2–3 cohorts)

**Purpose:** Validate the curriculum, gather testimonials, build case studies.

**Price:** $500 per seat

**Terms:**
- Participants provide honest feedback after each session and at completion
- Agency State may use anonymized results, quotes, and outcomes as case studies
- Participants get the full intensive — same content, same support, same deliverables as full-price cohorts
- No recordings shared publicly without permission

**Class size:** 8–10 participants (smaller for more hands-on attention while refining the curriculum)

**Revenue per founding cohort:** $4,000–$5,000

**Goal:** Run 2–3 founding cohorts. Collect testimonials, refine the curriculum, identify where people get stuck, document before-and-after outcomes. This phase is about learning, not revenue.

---

### Standard Pricing (post-founding)

**Individual seat:** $1,500

**Early bird:** $1,200 (available until 2 weeks before cohort start or first 6 seats, whichever comes first)

**Class size:** 10–12 participants

**Revenue per cohort at standard pricing:** $15,000–$18,000

---

### Team Pricing

**2–3 seats from same company:** $1,300 per seat (13% discount)

**4–6 seats from same company:** $1,100 per seat (27% discount)

**Full team (7+):** Custom pricing — transitions into a private cohort or team implementation engagement at $8,000–$15,000 depending on scope.

**Rationale:** Teams buying multiple seats are more valuable than individual buyers — they implement together, hold each other accountable, and are more likely to convert to advisory or implementation clients. The discount reflects that and incentivizes the team sale over the individual sale.

---

### On-Demand Course

**Price:** $297–$497

**What's included:**
- All module videos (watch, build, explore format)
- Reference prompts and data files
- Access to shared community channel
- Monthly live Q&A session (no additional cost)

**Upsell:** On-demand buyers can apply their purchase price toward a live cohort seat. A $497 on-demand buyer pays $1,003 to join a live cohort ($1,500 - $497). This creates a natural funnel from low-ticket to high-ticket.

---

## Cohort Frequency

### Phase 1 (founding, months 1–3)
- 1 cohort per month
- 8–10 participants
- Focus on curriculum refinement

### Phase 2 (standard, months 4–8)
- 1–2 cohorts per month
- 10–12 participants
- Begin team sales outreach

### Phase 3 (scaled, months 9+)
- 2–3 cohorts per month (if demand supports)
- Add specialized cohorts by function (marketing-focused, ops-focused)
- Add private cohorts for individual companies
- Consider adding a co-facilitator for additional cohorts

## Pricing Principles

1. **Never free.** Free signals uncertainty. Even the founding cohort has a real price.

2. **Founding discount is explicit and time-limited.** "You're getting in early. This price won't exist after the third cohort." Creates urgency without being pushy.

3. **Team pricing incentivizes the higher-value sale.** A team of four at $1,100 each ($4,400) is better than four individuals at $1,500 each ($6,000) on paper — but the team is more likely to implement, more likely to convert to advisory, and more likely to refer other teams.

4. **On-demand is a funnel, not a substitute.** The live cohort is the core product. On-demand serves people who can't commit to a schedule, and converts a percentage of them to live buyers.

5. **Don't compete on price.** The closest comparable (Harold Dijkstra's Maven bootcamp) is $799 for a 2.5-week cohort targeting PMs. Agency State's cohort is more specialized, more practical, and includes follow-up support. $1,500 is justified by the outcome — participants leave with working tools and a methodology, not just knowledge.

6. **Raise prices when you have proof.** After 2–3 founding cohorts with testimonials and case studies, the standard price has evidence behind it. After 6+ cohorts, consider raising to $2,000.
